About EarthSpoke

EarthSpoke is a student-led environmental initiative focused on building long-term ecological responsibility through awareness, sustainability, and community-driven action.

Our Mission

To promote sustainable living, encourage tree plantation, reduce plastic dependence, and inspire students to protect the environment through informed choices.

What We Do

Tree Plantation

Organising plantation drives to restore green spaces and strengthen local ecosystems.

Sustainable Alternatives

Encouraging biodegradable and eco-friendly products to reduce environmental harm.

Awareness Initiatives

Conducting educational sessions and campaigns to promote environmental responsibility.

Get in Touch

We collaborate with students, schools, volunteers, and organisations working towards a sustainable future.
